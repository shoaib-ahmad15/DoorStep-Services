ABOUT US

DOORSTEP Services

DoorStep is an online home services platform. Launched in 2021, DoorStep currently operates in Delhi-NCR and its outskirts. The platform helps customers book reliable and high quality services-beauty treatments, massages, haircuts, home cleaning, handymen, appliance repair, painting, virus fumigation and more-delivered by trained professionals conveniently at home. DoorStep's vision is to empower millions of professionals across the town to deliver services at home like never experienced before.

DoorStep Company provides a platform that allows skilled and experienced professionals to connect with userslooking for specific services. All the professionals, though experienced and skilled, undergo intensive training modules before being allowed to list their services on platform. Once on the platform, our match-making algorithm identifies professionals who are closest to the user's requirements and available at the requested time and date.
